4. Advanced Requirements

4.1 Flexbox Layouts 
•	Navigation bar: display:flex with gap and align-items:center for horizontal nav with logo
•	Services section on About page: flex-wrap row of 3 cards with equal widths and gap

4.2 CSS Grid Layout 
•	Portfolio gallery: grid-template-columns: repeat(auto-fill, minmax(280px, 1fr)) for responsive masonry-style layout

4.3 Animation & Effects 
•	@keyframes fadeSlideIn: hero text animates in on page load (opacity 0->1, translateY 30px->0)
•	@keyframes pulse: CTA button subtle pulsing glow effect
•	CSS transitions (0.3s ease) on all interactive elements: nav links, buttons, cards
•	transform: scale(1.05) on portfolio images and service cards on :hover
•	box-shadow elevation change on :hover for depth effect

4.4 Selectors & Pseudo-Classes 
•	5+ selector types: element, .class, #id, [attribute], nav > a (child), h2 + p (adjacent sibling), input[type='email']
•	5+ pseudo-classes: :hover, :focus, :active, :first-child, :nth-child(odd), :checked, :required, :not()

4.5 Responsive Design
•	Mobile-first approach with breakpoints at 768px (tablet) and 480px (mobile)
•	Navigation collapses or stacks on small screens
•	Grid/flex columns reduce to 1 on mobile
